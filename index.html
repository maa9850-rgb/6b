The Cost of Waiting

A short scroll story made by maitha alshamsi

1) “I’ll do it tomorrow.”

It starts small: one task, one message, one decision… postponed.

Panel 1 — The first delay feels harmless.

2) Tomorrow turns into “next week.”

Waiting adds up. The longer it sits, the heavier it feels to start.

Panel 2 — The gap gets bigger… and starting gets harder.

3) The moment passes.

Sometimes, waiting doesn’t just delay life — it changes what’s possible.

Panel 3 — The cost of waiting is what you miss.
